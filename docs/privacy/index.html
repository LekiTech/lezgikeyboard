Privacy Policy for Lezgi Keyboard

Effective date: July 20, 2026

Lezgi Keyboard (“the App”) is developed by LekiTech.

Information Collection

Lezgi Keyboard is designed to protect your privacy. The keyboard processes text locally on your device to provide typing suggestions, emoji, stickers, and personalized learning.

The App does not:

  • collect personal information;
  • collect passwords;
  • collect credit card information;
  • transmit typed text to any server;
  • upload your personal dictionary;
  • sell your data;
  • use your keystrokes for advertising.

Local Storage

To provide personalized typing features, the App stores certain information only on your device, including:

  • learned words;
  • recent emoji;
  • keyboard preferences;
  • theme settings.

This information never leaves your device.

Internet Access

The App does not require an internet connection for normal typing and does not send typed content to external servers.

Stickers and Images

When you choose to insert a sticker, the App provides that sticker to the destination app using Android’s standard content-sharing mechanism. The App does not upload stickers or typed content to LekiTech servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Data Security

Personal typing data remains stored locally on your device. You can clear learned words from the keyboard settings.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with a revised effective date.
